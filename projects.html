Latest Project:

Huntsville Middle School FBLA Website

The website above was created by Kelvin Orduna for the purpose of the Community Service Project at Huntsville Middle School FBLA. The website was then entered into the "Multimedia and Website Development" FBLA-ML competitive event following the guidelines.  The website then won First Place at District VI (Northwest Arkansas) and First Place at the Arkansas State Leadership Conference. (Watch Video) The website is currently being rebuilt to be entered into FBLA's National Leadership Conference, however may still be viewed.

The Huntsville Middle School FBLA website was presented as an online showcase of the school's community service project. It presented animations, YouTube Videos, photos, quotes, forms, music, links, and is made up of nine pages.

The .1hsd.org domain was lended to us by Bailey Cotton, Director of Technology at Huntsville Schools for project purposes.
